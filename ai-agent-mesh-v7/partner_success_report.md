**Issue:** Architect certification has 17% failure rate (vs. 13% target)

**Root Cause:** Advanced policy design module too technical, insufficient hands-on labs

**Mitigation:**
- [ ] Redesign Module 10 (Advanced Policy Design) with more interactive labs
- [ ] Add office hours with Mesh OS architects (2x/week)
- [ ] Create "Architect Bootcamp" (3-day intensive)

**Target:** Reduce failure rate to <10% by Q1 2026

### Challenge 2: Regional Coverage Gaps

**Issue:** Limited partner coverage in APAC and EMEA (80% of partners in North America)

**Root Cause:** Partner recruitment focused on US/Canada initially

**Mitigation:**
- [ ] Recruit 5 APAC partners (Q1 2026)

### Challenge 3: Small Deal Sizes (Silver/Registered Tiers)
