## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens if I exceed my included agent hours?
**A:** You'll be charged overage fees at your tier's rate ($0.15, $0.12, or $0.08 per hour). We send usage alerts at 80%, 90%, and 100% of your limit, so no surprises. You can also upgrade to the next tier mid-month to reduce overage costs.

### Q: Can I change tiers mid-contract?
**A:** Yes! You can upgrade anytime (prorated credit applied). Downgrades are processed at your next renewal date.

### Q: Do you offer a free trial?
**A:** Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial (Starter tier, 25 agents). No credit card required. Contact sales@mesh-os.ai to get started.

### Q: What if I need more than 10,000 agents?
**A:** Contact our sales team for custom pricing. We support deployments of any size.

### Q: Are there setup fees?
**A:** No setup fees for Starter and Professional tiers. Enterprise tier includes complimentary onboarding ($25,000 value).

### Q: Can I pay via AWS/Azure/GCP Marketplace?
**A:** Yes! Mesh OS is available on AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, and GCP Marketplace. Use your existing cloud credits and consolidated billing.

### Q: Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?
**A:** Yes, we offer 30% discounts for qualified non-profits and educational institutions. Contact sales@mesh-os.ai for details.

### Q: What is your refund policy?
**A:** We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual contracts. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund your full payment.
