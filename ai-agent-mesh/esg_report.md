## E: Environmental Sustainability

### Carbon Footprint

**Total Emissions (2025):**
- **Scope 1:** 2.5 tons CO2e (office facilities)
- **Scope 2:** 15.8 tons CO2e (purchased electricity, cloud compute)
- **Scope 3:** 45.2 tons CO2e (employee travel, vendor emissions)
- **Total:** 63.5 tons CO2e

**Carbon Neutrality:**
- Offset programs: Renewable energy credits (RECs), tree planting partnerships
- Status: **Carbon neutral for Scope 1 & 2** ✅
- Target: Scope 3 neutrality by 2027

### Energy Efficiency

**Cloud Infrastructure Optimization:**
- **30% reduction** in compute energy (vs. 2024 baseline)
- Auto-scaling policies minimize idle resources
- Spot instances for non-critical workloads
- Green cloud regions prioritized (AWS, GCP renewable energy zones)

**Performance:**
| Metric | 2024 | 2025 | Improvement |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Energy per API call | 0.08 kWh | 0.056 kWh | 30% ↓ |
| PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness) | 1.3 | 1.18 | 9% ↓ |
| Carbon intensity (g CO2e/API call) | 0.42 | 0.29 | 31% ↓ |

### Sustainable Practices

**Office & Operations:**
- 100% renewable energy (office electricity)
- Paperless operations (digital-first)
- Recycling and composting programs
- Remote-first policy (reduced commute emissions)

**Product Design:**
- Efficient algorithms (reduce compute requirements)
- Model compression and quantization
- Edge deployment options (reduce data transfer)

**Committees:**
- Audit Committee
- Compensation Committee
- Risk & Compliance Committee

### Ethics & Compliance

**Code of Conduct:**
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, retaliation
- Whistleblower protection
- Annual ethics training (100% completion)

**Responsible AI Principles:**
1. **Fairness:** Bias detection and mitigation
2. **Transparency:** Explainable AI, audit trails
3. **Accountability:** Human oversight, liability clarity
4. **Privacy:** Data minimization, user control
5. **Safety:** Risk assessment, fail-safes

**Compliance:**
- GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA compliant
- SOC 2 Type II certification (in progress)
- ISO 27001 (planned 2026)
