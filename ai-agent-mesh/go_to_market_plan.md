## 2. Sales Methodology

### 2.1 Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Firmographic:**
- **Industry:** Financial services, healthcare, insurance, technology
- **Size:** 500-10,000 employees
- **Revenue:** $100M-$5B annually
- **IT Budget:** $10M+ annually

**Technographic:**
- **AI Adoption:** 15+ active AI agents/copilots
- **Cloud:** AWS, Azure, or GCP presence
- **Identity:** Okta, Auth0, Azure AD
- **Observability:** Datadog, Splunk, New Relic

**Buying Triggers:**
- Failed SOC 2 audit (AI systems excluded)
- Shadow AI discovery by security team
- EU AI Act compliance deadline approaching
- CFO mandate to reduce AI spend by 20%+
- CISO board presentation on AI risk

### 2.2 Sales Playbook

**Discovery Questions:**
1. "How many AI agents/copilots are currently deployed?" (quantify sprawl)
2. "What percentage of prompts do you audit today?" (expose gaps)
3. "Can you attribute AI costs to business units?" (cost visibility)
4. "Have you had any AI security incidents?" (pain point)
5. "What's your timeline for [SOC 2 / ISO 42001 / EU AI Act]?" (urgency)

**Demo Flow:**
1. **Discovery Scan:** Show live agent detection (wow moment)
2. **Policy Enforcement:** Demonstrate prompt injection block
3. **Cost Dashboard:** Display 40% savings projection
4. **Compliance Report:** Generate audit-ready documentation
5. **Integration:** Connect to Okta + Datadog in <5 minutes

**Objection Handling:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already use Datadog" | "Datadog monitors, we govern. Can Datadog enforce RBAC on prompts? Block PII? We complement your APM." |
| "Too expensive" | "At $120K, you'll save $288K in compute costsâ€”2.4x ROI in year one." |
| "Not ready for AI governance" | "You're already doing it manually. We automate what you spend 10 hours/week on today." |
| "Need to wait for budget cycle" | "Start with a pilot now ($25K), prove ROI, scale in Q2 with full budget approval." |

### 2.3 Sales Compensation

**AE Compensation:**
- **Base:** $120K
- **Variable:** $80K (at 100% quota = $1M ARR)
- **Accelerators:** 1.5x over 110% quota, 2x over 150%
- **Quota:** $1M ARR per AE per year

**SE Compensation:**

- **Bonus:** $30K (tied to customer satisfaction + renewals)

**CSM Compensation:**

## 6. Customer Success & Retention

### 6.1 Onboarding Journey

**Week 1-2: Discovery & Setup**
- Kickoff call (CSM + customer stakeholders)
- Deploy discovery daemon
- Review agent inventory (expect 20-30% more than customer knew)
- Document current state

**Week 3-4: Policy Configuration**
- Map organizational roles to RBAC policies
- Configure PII redaction rules
- Set up rate limiting per business unit
- Integrate with Okta/Auth0

**Week 5-6: Integration & Validation**
- Connect observability tools (Datadog, Grafana)
- Set up alerting (Slack, PagerDuty)
- Run policy tests on 10% of traffic
- Measure baseline (costs, compliance coverage)

**Week 7-8: Full Rollout**
- Enable governance for 100% of agents
- Train customer team (admins, developers)
- Deliver 30-day report (savings, violations, recommendations)
- Schedule QBR (quarterly business review)

- Product Usage: 40 points
  - Daily active users >80%: 10 pts

  - NPS >50: 10 pts

**Intervention Triggers:**
