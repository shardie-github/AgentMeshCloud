## Executive Summary

The AI-Agent Mesh platform offers multiple monetization streams targeting enterprises seeking AI governance, compliance, and operational excellence. This document outlines pricing tiers, revenue models, and go-to-market strategies.

**Projected Revenue Streams:**
- **SaaS Subscriptions:** $50M ARR by Year 3
- **Professional Services:** $15M ARR by Year 3
- **Marketplace Revenue:** $5M ARR by Year 3
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $12B by 2028

## 1. Pricing Tiers

### 1.1 Developer Edition (Free)
**Target:** Individual developers, open-source projects, startups (<10 employees)

**Features:**
- Up to 5 registered agents
- 10,000 requests/month
- Basic policy enforcement (PII redaction, rate limiting)
- Community support
- Public GitHub issues

**Monetization:**
- Free forever
- Conversion funnel to paid tiers
- Community advocacy and brand awareness

---

### 1.2 Team Edition ($499/month)
**Target:** Small teams (10-50 employees), tech-forward SMBs

**Features:**
- Up to 25 registered agents
- 500,000 requests/month
- Full policy suite (RBAC, content safety, compliance)
- Basic telemetry and dashboards
- Email support (48-hour SLA)
- 99.5% uptime SLA

**Additional Costs:**
- +$50/month per additional 100K requests
- +$99/month per 10 additional agents

**Annual Discount:** 15% (= $5,085/year, save $915)

---

### 1.3 Business Edition ($2,499/month)
**Target:** Mid-market enterprises (50-500 employees), regulated industries

**Features:**
- Up to 100 registered agents
- 5M requests/month
- Advanced compliance (GDPR, SOC 2 adapters)
- Drift monitoring and auto-remediation
- Context federation and knowledge sharing
- Advanced analytics and custom dashboards
- Priority support (24-hour SLA)
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Dedicated customer success manager

**Additional Costs:**
- +$0.10 per 1,000 additional requests
- +$50/month per 5 additional agents

**Annual Discount:** 20% (= $23,990/year, save $5,998)

---

### 1.4 Enterprise Edition (Custom Pricing)
**Target:** Large enterprises (500+ employees), Fortune 500, government

**Pricing:** Starting at $10,000/month (typically $25K-$100K/month)

**Features:**
- Unlimited agents
- Unlimited requests
- On-premises / hybrid deployment
- White-label options
- Custom integrations
- Multi-region support
- Advanced security (SSO, mTLS, custom encryption)
- Premium support (2-hour SLA, dedicated Slack channel)
- 99.99% uptime SLA with credits
- Quarterly business reviews
- Custom SLA and MSA

**Add-Ons:**
- Professional services (implementation, training)
- Managed operations
- Custom policy development
- Compliance certification assistance (ISO 42001, etc.)

**Contract:** Annual or multi-year commitment

## 10. Pricing Strategy Principles

1. **Value-Based Pricing:** Price based on customer value (cost savings, compliance, risk reduction), not cost-plus
2. **Land and Expand:** Start with Team Edition, expand to Business/Enterprise as usage grows
3. **Transparent Pricing:** Public pricing tiers build trust; custom Enterprise pricing for flexibility
4. **Predictable Costs:** Flat monthly pricing with optional usage-based for predictability
5. **Low Friction:** Self-service signup for Team Edition, sales-assisted for Business+
6. **Competitive Parity:** Price at or below competitors with superior features
7. **Annual Incentives:** Encourage annual contracts with discounts for cash flow and retention

## 14. Pricing FAQs

**Q: Can I switch between tiers mid-contract?**  
A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades at renewal.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my request limit?**  
A: Requests are throttled (503 errors) unless you enable auto-overage billing.

**Q: Do you offer free trials?**  
A: Yes. 14-day trial for Team/Business editions (no credit card required).

**Q: Can I negotiate Enterprise pricing?**  
A: Yes. Contact sales for custom quote.

**Q: Are there setup fees?**  
A: No setup fees for Team/Business. Enterprise may have implementation fees.
