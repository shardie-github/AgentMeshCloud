## The EAGSP Category: Why Now?

### The Problem: Agent Proliferation Without Governance

Enterprises are deploying AI agents at unprecedented velocity:
- **Vertical SaaS agents** (Salesforce Einstein, ServiceNow Agent, HubSpot AI)
- **Custom-built agents** (fraud detection, trading, supply chain optimization)
- **Generative AI agents** (LLM-powered research, content generation, code assistants)

**Result:** A "Wild West" of uncoordinated automation with:
- **Conflicting objectives** (pricing agent A vs. pricing agent B)
- **Policy drift** (agent trained on Q1 rules, now operating in Q4)
- **Cascading failures** (one agent's error triggers 10 others)
- **Compliance blindness** (no audit trail, no explainability)

### The Gap: Existing Tools Don't Address Agent-Level Governance

| Tool Category | What It Does | What It Misses |
|---------------|--------------|----------------|
| **AIOps (Datadog, Dynatrace)** | Infrastructure monitoring | Agent behavior, policy enforcement |
| **Observability (Splunk, Grafana)** | Logs, metrics, traces | Orchestration, synchronization |
| **Workflow (Temporal, Airflow)** | Task execution | Trust metrics, compliance |
| **AI Governance (Manual frameworks)** | Static policies | Real-time enforcement, automation |

**Gap:** No unified platform for **agent-specific** visibility, orchestration, policy enforcement, and trust quantification.

### The Solution: EAGSP Category

**Enterprise Agent Governance and Synchronization Platform** provides:
1. **Real-Time Visibility** – Health, drift, alignment of every agent
2. **Policy Enforcement** – Continuous validation against SLAs, ethics, compliance
3. **Autonomous Orchestration** – Conflict detection + resolution without human intervention
4. **Trust Quantification** – Board-ready metrics (Trust Score, Risk Avoided, ROI)
5. **Audit Automation** – Immutable trails for SOC 2, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF
