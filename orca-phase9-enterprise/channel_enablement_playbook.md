**Program Tiers:**
- **Platinum Partners** (8 current) – 30% margin, co-sell, MDF $100K+

- **Silver Partners** (5 current) – 15% margin, basic enablement

- **Partner-Influenced Revenue:** $58.7M (76% of total revenue)
- **Avg Deal Size (Partner):** $380K vs. $290K (direct)

**2026 Goals:**

- 60% partner-sourced revenue target
- $100M+ in partner-influenced pipeline

## 3. Technical Enablement

### 3.1 Implementation Methodology

**ORCA Rapid Start (2-4 Weeks)**

**Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1)**
- Agent inventory (types, owners, criticality)
- Integration mapping (Datadog, Temporal, etc.)
- Compliance requirements (SOC 2, ISO 42001, etc.)

**Deliverable:** Discovery report + implementation plan

**Phase 2: Pilot Deployment (Weeks 2-3)**
- Deploy ORCA Control Plane (SaaS, Hybrid, or On-Prem)
- Instrument 5-10 pilot agents
- Configure baseline policies
- Integrate with observability stack

**Deliverable:** Pilot environment live, Trust Score baseline captured

**Phase 3: Validation (Week 4)**
- Run pilot for 1-2 weeks
- Validate Trust Score accuracy
- Test self-healing scenarios
- Executive business review (present results)

**Deliverable:** Go/No-Go decision for production rollout

---

**ORCA Full Deployment (4-12 Weeks)**

**Phase 1: Production Rollout (Weeks 1-4)**
- Expand to all production agents (50-500+ agents typical)
- Configure production policies (alignment, SLA, audit)
- Enable compliance automation (SOC 2, ISO 42001, etc.)

**Phase 2: Optimization (Weeks 5-8)**
- Tune Trust Score thresholds (per-agent risk levels)
- Enable advanced features (predictive drift, federated governance)
- Train customer ops teams (hands-on workshops)

**Phase 3: Operationalization (Weeks 9-12)**
- Establish ORCA Center of Excellence (CoE)
- Document runbooks (incident response, policy updates)
- Quarterly business review (QBR) cadence

**Deliverable:** ORCA fully operational, customer self-sufficient

### 4.3 Lead Sharing & Attribution

**Lead Routing Rules:**
1. **Partner-Originated Lead:** Partner gets 100% (ORCA supports as needed)
2. **ORCA-Originated Lead:** ORCA routes to partner if:
   - Customer in partner's territory (defined in agreement)
   - Partner has prior relationship with customer
   - Partner registered the account (deal registration)

**Deal Registration:**
- Partner registers opportunity in portal (within 7 days of discovery)
- ORCA validates (no existing engagement, no conflict with other partners)
- Protection period: 60-180 days (tier-dependent)

**Conflict Resolution:**
- If 2+ partners claim same opportunity → First to register wins
- If ORCA + Partner both engaged → Split credit (partner gets margin, ORCA gets direct sale credit)

## 5. Renewal & Expansion

### 5.1 Partner Role in Renewals

**Year 1 Renewal (Partner Responsibility)**
- **Timeline:** 90 days before contract end
- **Activities:**
  - Quarterly Business Review (QBR) with customer
  - Trust Score review (are they achieving targets?)
  - Expansion opportunities (additional agents, advanced features)
- **Margin:** Full partner margin (20-30%) on renewal

**Year 2+ Renewals (Shared Responsibility)**
- **ORCA CSM** (Customer Success Manager) owns relationship
- **Partner** supports (technical optimization, new use cases)
- **Margin:** Reduced partner margin (10-15%) on auto-renewals

**Expansion Opportunities:**
- Additional agents (10-50% more agents typical in Year 2)
- New use cases (start with fraud detection, expand to pricing agents)
- Advanced features (predictive drift, federated governance – add-ons)

---

## 6. Support & Escalation

### 6.1 Partner Support Tiers

**Tier 1: Partner-Led Support**
- **Scope:** Basic troubleshooting (configuration, policy updates, dashboard navigation)
- **SLA:** Partner-defined (recommended: 4 hours response)
- **Escalation:** If unresolved in 24 hours → Tier 2

**Tier 2: ORCA Partner Support**
- **Scope:** Technical issues (integration failures, Trust Score anomalies, performance)
- **SLA:** 4 hours (business hours), 1 hour (P1 severity)
- **Channel:** Partner support portal + dedicated Slack channel

**Tier 3: ORCA Engineering Escalation**
- **Scope:** Platform bugs, feature requests, architecture reviews
- **SLA:** 24 hours (platform bugs), 1 week (architecture reviews)
- **Channel:** Partner Success Manager (PSM) escalates to engineering

---

### 6.2 Common Escalation Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Trust Score Unexpectedly Low**
**Cause:** Usually policy misconfiguration or data quality issue  
**Resolution:** Partner reviews policy definitions → ORCA validates Trust Score calculation  
**SLA:** 4 hours (Tier 2)

**Scenario 2: Integration Failure (Datadog, Temporal, etc.)**
**Cause:** API credentials expired, network firewall blocking telemetry  
**Resolution:** Partner checks credentials/network → ORCA validates integration health  
**SLA:** 4 hours (Tier 2)

**Scenario 3: Agent Not Appearing in Dashboard**
**Cause:** Agent not instrumented correctly (SDK issue)  
**Resolution:** Partner reviews SDK integration → ORCA provides debugging guidance  
**SLA:** 4 hours (Tier 2)

## 10. Next Steps for New Partners
