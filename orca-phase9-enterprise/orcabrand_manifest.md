## Category Definition

**ORCA defines the Enterprise Agent Governance and Synchronization Platform (EAGSP) category.**

### What is an EAGSP?

An EAGSP is a unified control plane that:
1. **Monitors** agent behavior, workflows, and inter-agent dependencies in real time
2. **Enforces** policy compliance, SLA adherence, and ethical boundaries
3. **Orchestrates** adaptive synchronization to prevent drift and cascading failures
4. **Reports** trust metrics, ROI, and risk avoided to executive stakeholders
5. **Automates** self-healing, anomaly remediation, and audit preparation

Unlike traditional AIOps (focused on infrastructure) or observability tools (focused on logs/metrics), EAGSP addresses the **governance gap** in agent-driven automation.
